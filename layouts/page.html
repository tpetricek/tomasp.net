Tomas Petricek

Searching for new ways of thinking in programming & working with data

I believe that the most interesting work is not that of solving hard problems, but that of changing how we think about the world. I follow this belief in my work on data science tools, functional programming and F# teaching, in my programming languages research and I try to understand it through philosophy of science.

The Gamma

I'm working on making data-driven storytelling easier, more open and reproducible at the Alan Turing Institute.

Consulting

I've authored definitive F# books and open-source libraries. I offer my F# training and consulting services as part of fsharpWorks.

Academic

I've published papers on the theory of context-aware programming languages and type providers, but also on the philosophy of science.
